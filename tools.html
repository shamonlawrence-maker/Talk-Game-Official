Tools & Resources

A curated collection of my favorite tools for teaching, learning, and creating.

Canva

An excellent, user-friendly design tool for creating beautiful classroom materials, presentations, and student worksheets. The free version is incredibly powerful, and the pro version is free for educators.

Parlay

A fantastic platform for structuring and managing classroom discussions. It helps ensure all voices are heard and provides a framework for respectful, academic dialogue both in-person and online.

Notion

My personal go-to for curriculum planning, note-taking, and organizing research. It's a flexible workspace that can be adapted for almost any organizational need.
