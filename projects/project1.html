Project: Historical Inquiry on Ancient Civilizations

An 8-week, inquiry-based unit for 6th grade.

Overview

This unit was designed to move students beyond memorizing facts about ancient civilizations. Instead, students become historians, developing their own questions, analyzing primary sources, and constructing historical arguments. The unit culminates in a "Museum of History" where students present their findings to the community.

Learning Objectives

  • Students will be able to formulate a testable historical question.
  • Students will be able to analyze and interpret primary and secondary sources.
  • Students will be able to construct a evidence-based argument in writing.
  • Students will be able to collaborate with peers to present their findings.
