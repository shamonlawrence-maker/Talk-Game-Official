Teaching from the Desk: A Curriculum for Reclaiming Self in the Classroom

A 4-week interdisciplinary curriculum blending poetry, identity, and reflection for middle school learners.

Overview

This curriculum is designed around identity, memory, resistance, and legacy. Using poetry and reflective writing, students explore personal narratives and connect them to broader social and cultural contexts. Each week incorporates guided exercises, meditative prompts, and collaborative discussions that help students reclaim their voice in the classroom.

Learning Objectives

  • Develop personal and reflective writing skills
  • Analyze and respond to literary and poetic texts
  • Build classroom community through shared storytelling
  • Critically examine systemic education structures and personal agency
