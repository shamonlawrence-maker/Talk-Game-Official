English and Writing Lab: College Essay Curriculum

A three-week intensive course guiding students to craft college personal statements.

Overview

This course blends close reading, narrative structure, grammar instruction, and individualized feedback to help students produce polished personal statements for college applications. Students engage in daily writing exercises, meditative prompts, and revision workshops, supported by scaffolded rubrics and grading contracts.

Learning Objectives

  • Draft and revise a personal statement with clarity and authenticity
  • Apply grammar, narrative, and style techniques to personal writing
  • Reflect deeply on personal experience to create compelling narratives
  • Receive and incorporate constructive peer and teacher feedback
