Classroom Humanities Game Concepts

Interactive games designed to teach history, civics, and critical thinking through engagement and play.

Overview

I’ve developed multiple game concepts for middle school classrooms that align with humanities standards. These include map-based explorations, scenario-based debates, and role-play exercises designed to make history and civics tangible and memorable. Each game encourages student participation, problem-solving, and collaborative learning.

Learning Objectives

  • Build historical and civic understanding through play
  • Promote critical thinking and perspective-taking
  • Enhance collaboration and discussion in classroom settings
