Accountable Talk Stems Game

A classroom game to promote discussion skills, critical thinking, and student accountability.

Overview

This interactive game uses structured “talk stems” to help students engage in accountable discussions. Students practice citing evidence, elaborating on ideas, and responding respectfully to peers. The game aligns with middle school humanities standards and encourages a culture of curiosity and rigorous conversation.

Learning Objectives

  • Strengthen discussion and debate skills
  • Encourage evidence-based reasoning
  • Foster classroom community and inclusive participation
